Shipping & Returns Policy

We at Prachi’s Organics are committed to delivering your products safely and promptly. Please take a moment to review our shipping and return policies before placing your order.

Processing & Delivery Timelines (India)

Prepaid Orders: Dispatched within 5 business days Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders: Dispatched within 5 business days (after quick verification) Estimated Delivery Time: Metro Cities: 5 – 7 business days Remote Areas & Villages: 10 – 12 business days Delivery delays may occur during peak seasons, natural calamities, or due to unforeseen courier issues. We appreciate your understanding.

International Shipping

Processing Time: 3 business days Delivery Time: 10 – 15 business days Note: Customs duties, import taxes, or additional charges may apply as per your country’s regulations. These are the responsibility of the customer.

When: Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM Who: Someone must be available to receive and sign for your Prachi’s Organics order. If you’re unavailable, please authorize a trusted friend, family member, or neighbor to receive it on your behalf.

Returns & Replacements

We value your trust and maintain strict hygiene and quality control for every product shipped. Products Cannot Be Returned Once Delivered Due to safety and hygiene standards, we do not accept returns once the product has been delivered.

If Your Order Arrives Damaged

If your order reaches you in a damaged or defective condition, please inform us within 24 hours of delivery. To qualify for a replacement or refund: You must email us at support@prachisorganics.com within 24 hours of delivery. Include your Order ID and a clear unboxing video showing the damage. Once verified, we’ll initiate a replacement or refund within 4 – 7 business days to your original payment method. Please Note: Requests made after 24 hours of delivery or without unboxing video proof will not be eligible for replacement or refund. Damages due to improper use, mishandling, or negligence after delivery are not covered.
