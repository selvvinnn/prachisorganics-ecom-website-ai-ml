Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for a full refund. After 2 hours, a 30% deduction applies for prepaid orders (to cover processing and packaging costs). Once an order has been dispatched or shipped, cancellation or refund is not possible. To cancel an order, please email support@prachisorganics.com with your order details as soon as possible.

Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 4 – 7 business days to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, or UPI). Refund timelines may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
